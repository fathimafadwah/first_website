## List of pages

- Home page: Basic landing page with a hero image, columns with content and testimonials.
- Services page: Includes the services provided by the company and a comparison table comparing all the services.
- Team page: Contains the different teams in the company and their informations.
- About page: It contains the history of the compnay and it values.
- Contact page: This page has a form to contact the team and also a map for the location.
